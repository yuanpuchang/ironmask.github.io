Mercury Visit

  • -173°C to 427°C/-279.4 to 800.6°F
  • 5% the mass of Earth
  • 38% the size of Earth (The smallest planet)
  • 7 times brighter than Earth
  • No moon
  • Type: Rocky planet
  • The distance to Earth: 77-222 million km
  • Atmosphere: Instead of an atmosphere, Mercury possesses a thin exosphere composed of O2, Na, H2, He, and K.

Venus Visit

  • Approximately 460°C/860°F (The hottest planet)
  • 82% the mass of Earth
  • 95% the size of Earth
  • No moon
  • Type: Rocky planet
  • The distance to Earth: 38-261 million km
  • Only rotates clockwise (called retrograde rotation)
  • Atmosphere: mostly CO2, with clouds composed of H2S04
  • Strong greenhouse effect

Earth

  • -89.2 to 56.7°C/-128.56 to 134.06°F
  • Mass: 5.97*1024kg
  • Size: 12,742 km radius
  • 1 moon: the Moon
  • Type: Rocky planet
  • Atmosphere: consists of 78% N2, 21% O2, and 1% other gases

Mars Visit

  • -140 to 30°C/-220 to 86°F
  • 10% the mass of Earth
  • 50% the size of Earth
  • 2 moons: Phobos & Deimos
  • Type: Rocky planet
  • The distance to Earth: 55.7-401 million km
  • Seasons like Earth
  • Atmosphere: CO2, N2, Ar

Jupiter Visit

  • -161 to -108°C/-257.8 to -162.4 °F
  • 318 times the mass of Earth
  • 11 times the size of Earth
  • 79 moons
  • Type: Gas giant
  • The distance to Earth: 588-928 milloin km
  • No solid surface
  • Atmosphere:
    • The top cloud: ammonia ice
    • The middle layer: [NH4]SH
    • The innermost layer: water, ice and vapor

Saturn Visit

  • -189 to -139°C/-308.2 to -218.2°F
  • 95 times the mass of Earth
  • 9 times the size of Earth
  • 146 moons
  • Type: Gas giant
  • The distance to Earth: 1.2-1.7 billion km
  • No solid surface
  • Atmosphere: blanketed with clouds that appear as faint stripes, jet streams, and storms.

Uranus Visit

  • -226 to -197°C/-374.8 to -322.6°F
  • 15 times the mass of Earth
  • 4 times the size of Earth
  • Type: Ice giant
  • The distance to Earth: 2.58-3.15 billion km
  • Inner rins are darker; outer ring are brighter colored
  • No solid surface
  • Atmosphere: mostly of H2 and He2 with just a little bit of CH4 and NH3

Neptune Visit

  • -218 to -200 °C
  • 4 times the mass of Earth
  • 17 times the size of Earth
  • Type: Ice giant
  • The distance to Earth: 4.32 - 4.17 billion km
  • The only planet in solar system not visible to the naked eye
  • Atmosphere: mostly of H2 and He2 with just a little bit of CH4
  • Neptune's blue colour is caused by the CH₄ found in its atmosphere

(No to scale)
